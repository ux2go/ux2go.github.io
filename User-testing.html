User Testing

Having spent over 1,000 hours running UX evaluations across fintech, e-commerce, education, telecommunication, and healthcare, UX2GO is always ready to dive into sessions that give a voice to your users. Depending on the project, we can have the entire evaluation wrapped up in as little as 5 days. Still too long? Have a look at our Heuristic Review service.

How it works

1. Familiarisation and preparation

        • First conversation, introductions, and communication of brief
        • Research plan, recruitment screener, and discussion guide submitted as drafts for approval (1 revision cycle)
        • Recruitment is kicked off and the research schedule is populated

2. Sessions

Sessions are conducted remotely via webcam and screen sharing (when prototypes are in use). Session lengths are around 30 or 60 minutes dependent on project requirements. Throughout the session, the moderator (the interviewer) will follow the agreed upon discussion guide. All session recordings are stored in accordance with GDPR.

3. Data analysis and write-up

        • Raw data is compiled and reviewed
        • A presentation deck is constructed outlining key insights and pain points, which are in turn graded in terms of severity

4. Delivery

What you get:

        • A full write-up and analysis in the form of a presentation deck
        • A breakdown of key pain points, each graded against UX2GO's severity scale
        • A raw data file typically in the form of a spreadsheet
        • A copy of the discussion guide used in the sessions
        • Links to the session recordings (upon request)
        • Recommendations for next steps
