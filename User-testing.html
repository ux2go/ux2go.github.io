User Testing

Having spent over 1,000 hours running UX evaluations across fintech, e-commerce, education, telecommunication, and healthcare, UX2GO is always ready to dive into sessions that gives a voice to the user.

How the sessions are run

Sessions are conducted remotely via webcam and screen sharing (when prototypes are in use). Potential participants are screened to ensure that they represent the target users and are allocated a slot of either 30 or 60 minutes dependent on project requirements. Throughout the session, the moderator (the interviewer) will follow a discussion guide tailored to your needs. All session recordings are stored in accordance with GDPR.

Deliverables

Following the sessions, UX2GO will then analyse the data and put together a final package to be delivered to you. This package consists of:

        • a full write-up and analysis in the form of a presentation deck
        • a raw data file typically in the form of a spreadsheet
        • recommendations for next steps
        • a copy of the discussion guide used in the sessions
        • (If requested) Links to the session recordings.
