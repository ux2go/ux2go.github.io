What is UX2GO?

UX2GO is a service that was created with the aim of providing actionable insights to those looking for a cost-effective and time-saving solution to improving the UX of their digital interfaces.


What do we do?

Over the last few years, UX research has gained more and more recognition as a vital part of the design process. However, we know that it can be hard to find the time and resources to carry it out effectively and impartially.

With strong roots in psychology and experience leading UX research activities across a wide range of industries (healthcare, finance, education, e-commerce, and telecommunication), UX2GO is primed to undertake research activities that are guaranteed to improve your understanding of intended users and the prospects of your designs.


How does it work?

As UX2GO is an online service, it doesn't matter where you are in the world. UX2GO comes to you. And it's very simple. You can see when UX2GO has available slots on the calendar page and contact us to book a project. Normally, we will then look to set up a call to answer any questions and iron out the details. We then start the project on the agreed upon date, providing updates as necessary. By end-of-play on the last day of the project, you can expect a package containing:

        • a full write-up and analysis in the form of a presentation deck
        • any raw data collected
        • recommendations for next steps.


How long is a project?

How long is a piece of string? No two projects are the same but typically you can expect a standard round of user testing (which includes writing an interview script, recruitment, running 6 remote user interviews, data analysis, and write-up) to take between 5 and 8 working days dependent on user requirements. Other services, such as heuristic reviews, typically require a shorter time to complete (2 to 3 days) as there are no recruitment needs.
