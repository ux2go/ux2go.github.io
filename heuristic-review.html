Heuristic review

Heuristic reviews (a form of expert review) are an excellent and efficient way to evaluate the UX and usability of websites and apps without spending too much time and budget. We use the UX2GO Heuristic Review Tool to generate insights, rank and grade usability issue severity, propose recommendations, and put forward a review that is objective. Depending on the project, we can carry out the entire review in as little as 2 days.

How it works

The UX2GO Heuristic Review Tool

We are very proud of our heuristic review tool, which is the culmination 4 years of careful refinement and iteration. Originally drawing upon Jakob Nielsen's 10 usability heuristics (1994) and medical uFMEA (use failure mode effects analysis) formatting, our tool has evolved to modernise criteria, make things simpler, and render our assessments more systematic and objective. It has been used to assist UX researchers in assessing the usability of websites and apps since 2015 and is fine tuned to pick out the root causes underlying UX issues and provide strong foundations on which to move forward.

Process

It's a straightforward process. After discussing with you the nature of the project at hand we just need the website or app, along with any specifics such as a single user journey. We will then set about running it through our review tool. Typically, a heuristic review cycle will last 2 to 3 days depending on the particulars of the project.

Delivery

What you get:

        • The full output of our Heuristic Review Tool in the form of a carefully formatted spreadsheet
        • A copy of our 10 heuristics, their definitions, and examples
        • Recommendations regarding how best to rectify usability issues uncovered in the review
